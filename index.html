WHO WE ARE ?

We are Error CS Team a team for Computer Science that started in 2018 in Faculty of Science MU founded by a punch of students who have the knowladge and experts in CS Fileds and Technology also worked on many projects and participated in many contest.

Our Goals:

  • Establish a powerful community able to learn and lead.
  • Help people to get CS skills which make them create their own projects from scratch.
  • Make people ready after graduation for jobs by preparing them for the long term on Computer's Fields.
  • Move members of the team to an advanced level of self-learning (Learn and Teach) technique which is giving them self confident and taking responsibilities for being such a leader of a group.
